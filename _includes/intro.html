LEARNING BY DOING

Kids Coding Camp helps from kids to teenagers to understand fundamental programming basically. We are aiming to help young children experience charming side of programming and creativity without any old-school teaching methodology.


PRACTICE, PRACTICE, PRACTICE

After years of professional careers, we realized the easiest and most efficient way of learning is PRACTICE. Programming is basically a training for brain and logic thinking, which helps the children for all future possibilities in career, in self-organization, in rational thinking. Kids Coding Camp wants to bring the learning programming process through projects based, through more creativity ways. The children could create games, apps, websites, AI and more.


CURRICULUMS

We have different groups for kids and teenagers. Kids will be trained specific for logic thinking and creativity through basic programming projects. Teenagers will focus on more real world programming problems, learning one real world programming language and start multiple praxis based projects.
