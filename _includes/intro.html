LEARNING BY DOING

Kids Coding Camp helps from kids to teenagers understand fundamental programming basically. We are aiming to help young children experience charming side of programming, creativity without any old-school teaching methodology.


PRACTICE, PRACTICE, PRACTICE

After years of professional career, we realized the easiest and most efficient way of learning is practice. Programming is basically a training for brain and logic thinking, which helps the children for all future possibilities in career, in self-organization, in rational thinking. Kids Coding Camp want to bring the learning programming though projects, through more creativity ways. The children could create games, apps, websites, AI and more.
